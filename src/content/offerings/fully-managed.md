## Fully Managed

ConcertIM takes **instant (9–5) alert response**, **owns**
the environment's health, and delivers a standardised, tried-and-tested HPC
stack built on two decades of real engineering through our Alces partnership.

You don't need to worry about the operating system; you don't chase alerts; 
you don't own the downtime risk. Your team spends its time on **service delivery**, 
not on the platform underneath it.

**Best for** teams that want peace of mind and a guaranteed baseline, with
the platform entirely off their plate.
