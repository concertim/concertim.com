## Who we are

ConcertIM delivers HPC and AI services as a subscription. We're **not** a
consultancy: we run platforms. The difference is what you decide to put in
our hands — from a fully-managed service where we own the lot, to a
passive arrangement where you keep the keys and we simply keep watch.

## Backed by 20+ years

Our exclusive partnership with **Alces** brings two decades of real HPC
engineering behind every ConcertIM service. We've combined tried-and-tested
methodologies with a modern platform to build next-generation HPC and AI
services — without reinventing what already works.

## Built for sustainability

Every subscription is designed to cut the carbon cost of research
workloads: shared infrastructure, resources that get reallocated rather than
left idle, and meaningful carbon figures so you can see — and shrink — your
impact. Affordable, sustainable, and scalable by construction, not as an
afterthought.
