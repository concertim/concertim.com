## The five components

Every ConcertIM service is a combination of the same five modular
components. What changes between offerings is **who owns each one** — that's
the dial. Pick the combination that fits how hands-on you want to be.

## How to choose

- **Passive Reporting** — you run it; we watch and report.
- **Fully Managed** — we run it; you get results.

Not sure where you sit? [Tell us what you run](/contact) and we'll work out the
rest.
